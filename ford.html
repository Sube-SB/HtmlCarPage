FORD

Ford Motor Company is a publicly traded company owned by a diverse group of shareholders, but the founding Ford family retains significant control through a special class of stock, giving them a plurality of voting power despite owning less than 50% of the equity. Key shareholders also include large institutional investors like Vanguard and BlackRock, while the Ford family, led by William Clay Ford Jr., maintains influence through Class B shares, ensuring their long-term control.
